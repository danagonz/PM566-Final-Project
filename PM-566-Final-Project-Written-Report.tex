\subsubsection{Introduction}\label{introduction}

Hepatitis C virus (HCV) infection is the most common blood-borne
infection in the United States. National surveillance data has shown a
significant rise in acute HCV cases (those in the first 6 months of
infection) in the last decade, with case counts having nearly doubled.

\textbf{In this assignment, I will visualize trends in HCV incidence
rate for the racial/ethnic groups included in my chosen dataset, and
determine which group(s) have seen the greatest rise in incidence for
the time period of interest by calculating the percent change in
incidence from 2000 to 2022}.

This dataset includes the following five variables:

\begin{itemize}
\tightlist
\item
  ``Indicator'' (``Acute Viral Hepatitis C'' for all observations)
\item
  ``Year'' (2000-2022)
\item
  ``Race.Ethnicity''

  \begin{itemize}
  \tightlist
  \item
    ``American Indian/Alaska Native''
  \item
    ``Asian/Pacific Islander''
  \item
    ``Black/African American''
  \item
    ``Hispanic/Latino''
  \item
    ``White''
  \item
    ``Other''
  \item
    ``Unknown''
  \end{itemize}
\item
  ``Cases'' (crude case counts)
\item
  ``Rate.per.100.000'' (incidence rate)
\end{itemize}
